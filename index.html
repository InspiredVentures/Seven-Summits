A 3-Day Journey to Elevate Your Life

The Seven Summits Summit is more than just an event; it's a transformative experience designed to helping you set bold goals and conquer your personal summits.

Nestled in the breathtaking mountains of Sun Valley, you'll join a community of like-minded individuals for three days of world-class speakers, outdoor adventures, and deep connections.

  • Inspiring Keynotes
  • Guided Nature Excursions
  • Exclusive Networking

Meet Your Host:
Mark Pattison

From the NFL Gridiron to the Top of the World.

Mark Pattison is a former NFL player, philanthropist, and mountaineer who became the first NFL player to climb the Seven Summits.

After his football career, the mountains helped Mark find a new purpose. Now, he's bringing that same spirit of resilience and achievement to you. This event is his way of giving back and helping others find clarity and strength in the great outdoors.
